Smart Street light Project

My Smart Street Light Project uses a Raspberry Pi and an automated light sensor to efficiently control street lighting. The system automatically turns lights on when ambient light levels drop (e.g., at night or in foggy conditions) and turns them off when sufficient daylight is detected. This automation helps in energy conservation, reducing power wastage while ensuring proper illumination.
